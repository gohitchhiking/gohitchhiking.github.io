Introduction: Discovering the Charms of Tybee Island#

Tybee Island, a charming barrier island spanning 3.36 square miles, is located just 18 miles east of Savannah. In this quick tour, we will explore the captivating beauty and unique features of this small town, offering an abundance of activities and attractions for visitors. Whether you’re seeking relaxation on its pristine sandy beaches, immersing yourself in history, or indulging in sumptuous seafood, Tybee Island has something for everyone.

An Island Getaway: Where to Stay and Dine#

Tybee Island boasts a variety of accommodation options for every taste and budget. With eight hotels, six bed and breakfast inns, and over 800 houses, cottages, and condos, finding the perfect place to stay is a breeze. Immerse yourself in the warm Southern hospitality as you indulge in delicious local seafood at one of the island’s 25 casual dining restaurants.

Embrace the Small-Town Feel: Exploring the Island#

To truly experience the charm of Tybee Island, take a drive along the island’s major highway, US-80. This one-lane road encapsulates the small-town feel, allowing you to soak in the relaxed atmosphere and picturesque surroundings. As you journey along, you’ll discover three miles of sandy white beaches, divided into three distinct locations: North, Mid, and South. Each section offers a unique ambiance and breathtaking views of the Atlantic Ocean.

Uncover History: Landmarks and Attractions#

Tybee Island is steeped in history, boasting several fascinating landmarks. The island proudly houses the nation’s oldest lighthouse, built in 1736, offering visitors the chance to climb to the top for panoramic views of the island and surrounding waters. Additionally, the island is home to two forts and a historical theater, providing a glimpse into its rich past.

Fun in the Sun: Outdoor Beach Activities#

For outdoor enthusiasts, Tybee Island is a paradise. With its prime beachfront location, the island offers a plethora of activities to enjoy. Embark on thrilling adventures such as kayaking, canoeing, paddle boating, and fishing tours. Indulge in the refreshing ocean breezes as you bask in the sun-drenched shores of Tybee Island.

Acclaim and Awards: Recognizing Tybee Island’s Beauty#

Tybee Island has garnered numerous accolades from renowned publications and travel websites such as Travel + Leisure, Forbes online, Trivago magazines, and more. The island has been recognized as a quaint little small town, an ideal destination for affordable vacations, best spring breaks, and the ultimate summer beach town. Its natural beauty and charm have made it an iconic and beloved coastal area.

Conclusion: A Must-Visit Destination#

Our tour of Tybee Island has provided a glimpse into the wonders of this coastal gem. From its pristine beaches and rich history to its delectable seafood and small-town charm, Tybee Island is a must-visit destination for all. Whether you seek relaxation, outdoor adventure, or a journey through time, this island offers a memorable experience.
