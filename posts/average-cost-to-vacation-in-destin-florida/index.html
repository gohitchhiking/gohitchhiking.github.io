Destin, Florida is widely regarded as one of the premier vacation spots in the state. In this comprehensive guide, we will delve into everything you need to know about this beautiful city, including the best times to visit, money-saving tips, recommended places to stay, top-rated restaurants, must-visit attractions, and more. Whether you’re a seasoned traveler or a first-time visitor, this guide will ensure you make the most of your vacation in Destin.

Pre-Travel Recommendations#

Before embarking on your trip to Destin, it’s essential to have a good understanding of some pre-travel recommendations that will make your vacation even more enjoyable.

Location and Best Times to Visit#

Destin is situated along the Florida Panhandle, between Pensacola and Panama City Beach. While its location makes it easily accessible for visitors who don’t want to venture further south to areas like Tampa and Orlando, it’s worth noting that the city experiences slightly cooler weather in late fall, winter, and early spring compared to other tourist spots in Florida. For those looking to enjoy the water and pools, visiting in May and September is recommended. If you prefer the summer months, aim for the first two weeks of June or the last two weeks of August.

Money-Saving Tips#

To save money on your Destin vacation, consider visiting during the off-season. During this time, rates for hotels, condos, and vacation rentals are more affordable. You can make significant savings by avoiding the crowds and opting for the more budget-friendly options during this period.

Duration of Stay#

While Destin may be a small town, it offers plenty to see and do. To truly experience all that Destin has to offer, a minimum of three full days in the area is recommended. This will allow you to explore both the city and its beautiful beaches at a leisurely pace.

Why Destin?#

One of the main draws of Destin is its stunning sandy beaches. Renowned for having some of the best sand in Florida, Destin boasts clean and easily navigable beaches that appeal to all types of travelers. The city’s cleanliness, ease of navigation, and diverse range of attractions make it an ideal vacation destination.

Getting to Destin#

For those flying into Destin, the closest airport is Destin-Fort Walton Beach Airport, located just across the bay, approximately 16 miles away. Although there is no interstate that runs through Destin, reaching the city from the interstate is quick and straightforward.

Best Places to Stay#

Destin offers a plethora of accommodation options, catering to all budgets. Along the main stretch of Destin, you’ll find several well-known hotel chains. The great advantage is that 90% of hotels and condos in the area are within walking distance of the beach. If you prefer a more unique experience and want to make the most of your time in Destin, consider staying at one of the following highly recommended places:

  1. Sterling Shores Beach Resort (4.5 out of 5 stars on Google)
  2. Henderson Beach Resort (4.6 stars)
  3. Hidden Dunes Beach and Tennis Resort (4.6 stars)
  4. Beach Resort by Ocean Reef Resorts (4.6 stars)
  5. Destin Point (4.5 stars)

For those seeking higher affordability, it is advisable to avoid booking accommodations on Friday or Saturday nights, as these tend to be the most expensive.

Things to Do in the Destin Area#

The Destin area offers a wide range of activities and attractions to suit everyone’s interests. Here are some key highlights:

Golf Courses#

If you enjoy a round of golf, Destin has two exceptional courses that are well-maintained and conveniently located. Consider visiting:

  1. Emerald Bay Golf Club (4.5 rating on Google)
  2. Burnt Pine Golf Club (4.7 rating)

To save money on golf, take advantage of the cheaper rates offered during less busy tee times. Check the golf courses’ websites for any available discounts.

Family Attractions#

Families with children or those who love exploring attractions will be delighted by these must-visit destinations:

  1. Wild Willy’s Adventure Zone (4.3 stars)
  2. Gulfarium Marine Adventure Park (4.4 stars)
  3. Blue Water Zoo Exotic Aquariums and Reptiles (4.7 stars)
  4. The Track Adventure Park Destin (4.3 stars)
  5. Buccaneer Pirate Cruise (4.9 stars)

These family attractions offer quality entertainment and experiences that the whole family will enjoy.

Water Attractions#

For water enthusiasts, the Destin area has a wide array of options:

  1. Big Kahuna’s Water and Adventure Park (3.9 stars)
  2. Extreme H2O (4.7 stars)
  3. Destin Water Taxi (4.4 stars)
  4. Olin Marler Fishing Charters (4.9 stars)

For those on a budget, spending time at the beach is an excellent cost-saving option, as it is free and offers a wonderful experience.

Restaurants in Destin#

Destin is known for its culinary delights, with numerous restaurants catering to all tastes and budgets. Here are five highly recommended options:

  1. The Back Porch (4.4 out of 5 stars on Google)
  2. Dewey Destin’s Harborside (4.4 stars)
  3. Fudpucker’s Beachside Bar and Grill (4.3 stars)
  4. The Edge Seafood Restaurant and Sky Bar (4.4 stars)
  5. The Beach House Brew Pub (4.8 out of 5 stars)

While dining out can be expensive, you can save money by preparing breakfast and snacks in your accommodation and enjoying dinner at one of Destin’s fantastic restaurants.

Best Beaches in the Destin Area#

Henderson State Park stands out as the best overall beach in the Destin area, known for its pristine beauty and clear waters. Additionally, Norriego Point and Eglin Beach Park are highly recommended spots that offer fantastic beach experiences.

To save on parking fees, consider finding a hotel near the beach that does not charge for parking. This way, you can conveniently walk to the beach without incurring additional expenses.

Hurricane Season in Destin#

As the Florida Panhandle experiences its fair share of hurricanes, it’s important to keep this in mind if planning a trip between early June and October. Stay updated on weather forecasts and plan accordingly to ensure your safety.

Nightlife in Destin#

If you’re looking to spice up your nights and enjoy live entertainment, Destin offers a couple of exciting options:

  1. Coyote Ugly Saloon (3.9 out of 5 stars on Google)
  2. Club LA, a live entertainment venue (4.6 rating)

These venues provide excellent nightlife experiences and a chance to enjoy great entertainment.

Other Must-See Attractions#

While there is much more to discover in the Destin area, a few additional noteworthy attractions include:

  1. HarborWalk Village: A hub of dining, entertainment, and shopping, HarborWalk Village is a must-visit destination for all travelers to Destin.
  2. Ross Marler Park (4.6 rating) and Okaloosa Island Pier (4.6 rating) offer enjoyable experiences and are great options if you’re looking for free or low-cost attractions.

Shopping in Destin#

Destin offers an array of shopping opportunities, including the vibrant HarborWalk Village. Those looking for outlet stores must visit the Silver Sands Premium Outlets (4.5 rating) for a fantastic shopping experience. Destin Commons (4.6 rating) is another popular shopping destination, featuring a wide range of shops, restaurants, and even a movie theater.

Mini-Golf Courses#

Mini-golf is always a favorite family activity while on vacation, and Destin offers some excellent courses:

  1. Okaloosa National Mini Golf and Ice Cream (4.4 stars)
  2. Wild Willy’s Adventure Zone (4.3 stars)
  3. Mini Golf and Fat Daddy’s Arcade at The Track Destin (4.5 stars)

Playing mini-golf in the evening or at night can make the experience even more enjoyable, as you avoid the scorching daytime sun.

Indoor Activities for Rainy Days#

As Florida is prone to rain, it’s essential to have backup plans for indoor activities. In Destin, you can enjoy:

  1. Destin Laser Tag (4.5 rating)
  2. AMC Destin Commons for a movie (4.1 out of 5 rating)
  3. The Escape Room 60 (4.5 rating)

Alternatively, bring along a deck of cards or board games to enjoy in the comfort of your hotel room or condo when it rains.
