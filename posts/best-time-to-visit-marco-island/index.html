**THE MARCO ISLAND TRAVEL GUIDE (2023) | Discover the Best Time to Visit this Florida Island Beach Town**

July 31, 2023 · 3 min · 638 words · Jacqueline Chamberlain

Marco Island is a breathtaking destination located in Florida that offers a variety of activities and attractions for visitors to enjoy. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing beach vacation or an adventure-filled getaway, Marco Island has something to offer. In this travel guide, we’ll explore the best time to visit Marco Island, the must-see attractions, and the top things to do during your stay.

When is the Best Time to Visit Marco Island?

The best time to visit Marco Island is during the winter months, from December to February. This is the peak season for tourism in Florida, and Marco Island is no exception. The weather during these months is mild and pleasant, with temperatures ranging from the 70s to 80s Fahrenheit. This is the perfect time to enjoy the beautiful beaches and outdoor activities that Marco Island has to offer.

Exploring Marco Island

To make the most of your visit to Marco Island, it’s important to plan your itinerary and prioritize the attractions that interest you the most. Here are some of the top things to do during your stay:

  1. Tiger Tail Beach: Start your day at Tiger Tail Beach, the most popular beach on Marco Island. Enjoy the white sandy beaches and clear blue waters while taking a leisurely walk along the shore. Don’t forget to bring your beach gear and sunscreen for a day of relaxation and fun.

  2. Marco Island Marriott Beach Resort, Golf Club & Spa: Indulge in luxury at the Marco Island Marriott Beach Resort, Golf Club, and Spa. This four-star beachfront resort offers stunning views and an array of amenities, including a tiki bar and grill. Treat yourself to a delicious meal and enjoy the beachfront atmosphere.

  3. South Marco Beach: Take a short walk from your parking spot to South Marco Beach, where you’ll find beautiful white sand and a beachfront bar and grill. This is the perfect spot to catch a stunning sunset and unwind after a day of exploring.

  4. Dolphin Tiki Bar and Grill: Grab a drink and relax at the Dolphin Tiki Bar and Grill, a popular spot among locals and tourists alike. Enjoy happy hour specials and delicious cocktails while taking in the waterfront views.

  5. Sweet Annie’s Ice Cream: End your day with a sweet treat at Sweet Annie’s Ice Cream. With a wide variety of flavors to choose from, you’re sure to find something to satisfy your cravings. Don’t be discouraged by the line, as the ice cream here is well worth the wait.

Tips for a Successful Trip to Marco Island

  1. Pack sunscreen: The Florida sun can be intense, so it’s important to protect your skin from harmful UV rays. Be sure to pack sunscreen with a high SPF rating and apply it regularly throughout the day.

  2. Stay hydrated: With the warm temperatures in Marco Island, it’s essential to stay hydrated. Bring a reusable water bottle with you and fill it up throughout the day to avoid dehydration.

  3. Check the tide schedule: If you plan on visiting Tiger Tail Beach, check the tide schedule before heading out. Walking through the lagoon at low tide is the easiest and most convenient way to access the beach.

  4. Arrive early: To beat the crowds at popular attractions and restaurants, try to arrive early in the day. This will give you more time to explore and enjoy the sights without feeling rushed.

Conclusion

Marco Island is a hidden gem in Florida that offers a unique blend of natural beauty and modern amenities. Whether you’re seeking adventure or relaxation, Marco Island has it all. From pristine beaches to delicious dining options, this island paradise is waiting to be explored. Plan your visit during the winter months to make the most of your trip and create lifelong memories. Start packing your bags and get ready for an unforgettable experience on Marco Island!
