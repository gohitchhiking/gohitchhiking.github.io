Cozumel, Mexico: A DIY One Day Tour - Explore the Hidden Gems

Cozumel, a picturesque island in Mexico, offers a perfect getaway for those seeking adventure and relaxation. In this article, we will take you on a virtual tour of Cozumel and provide you with valuable insights and tips for planning your own one-day tour. From transportation options to hidden beaches and local eateries, we have it all covered!

Ultramar or Windjet: Choosing the Right Boat#

When traveling from Playa Del Carmen to Cozumel, there are two popular boat options available: Ultramar and Windjet. Both provide comfortable rides, but we opted for the Windjet boat. The tickets for both boats cost just under a thousand pesos for a round trip, and the departure time doesn’t correlate with the time on your ticket. As long as you arrive on time, you are good to go. The Windjet boat offered spacious seats and a variety of drinks and snacks for purchase during the journey.

Exploring Cozumel: Renting a Car and Beach Hopping#

After reaching Cozumel, we headed to a car rental place to get our wheels for the day. Despite a few quirks with the car, such as non-functioning mirrors and a rattling window, driving around the island was an exciting experience. The west side of Cozumel offered scenic views of lush jungles, while the east side promised sparkling beaches.

We wanted to keep our tour budget-friendly, so instead of opting for expensive beach clubs, we decided to visit public beaches. Our first stop was Playas Los Rocas, known for its rocky shoreline. Although the beach wasn’t ideal for swimming, it still offered a unique charm. However, we didn’t linger for too long and decided to find another public beach with better swimming opportunities.

A Memorable Lunch Experience#

While exploring Cozumel, we stumbled upon a hidden gem - a local restaurant tucked away from the touristy areas. It provided a quiet atmosphere and authentic local flavors. Jay enjoyed a delicious burrito, while I relished the taste of chicken fingers and fries with barbecue sauce. The restaurant offered a much-needed break from the hustle and bustle of tourist spots.

A Serendipitous Discovery: A Beach Worth Diving Into#

After lunch, we embarked on a quest to find the perfect beach, despite encountering a few wrong turns and construction obstacles. Finally, we found a beautiful public beach that exceeded our expectations. Crystal-clear waters and soft sands awaited us, making it the ideal spot for swimming and relaxing. We even tried using a waterproof phone bag to capture our underwater adventures, although we encountered some technical difficulties.

Tips for a Successful DIY Tour#

If you’re planning a one-day tour of Cozumel, here are some tips to ensure a smooth and memorable experience:

  1. Choose the Right Boat: Consider the Ultramar or Windjet boats for a comfortable ride to Cozumel.
  2. Rent a Car or Scooter: Having your own transportation allows you to explore the island at your own pace and venture off the beaten path.
  3. Visit Public Beaches: Save money and discover hidden gems by opting for public beaches instead of pricey beach clubs.
  4. Dine Like a Local: Seek out local restaurants away from tourist hotspots to savor authentic flavors and enjoy a quieter atmosphere.
  5. Embrace Serendipity: Don’t be discouraged by unexpected obstacles. Sometimes, the best experiences come from unplanned discoveries.
  6. Be Prepared: If you plan to capture underwater moments, ensure your waterproof accessories are fully functional and test them beforehand.
  7. Consider a Scooter: If you’re comfortable with two wheels, renting a scooter allows for easier parking and maneuvering through traffic.

Embrace the Magic of Cozumel#

Cozumel, with its breathtaking scenery and vibrant culture, offers a memorable experience for travelers. Whether you’re seeking adventure or tranquility, this island has something to offer. From exploring hidden beaches to indulging in local cuisine, you can create your own perfect one-day tour. So pack your bags, prepare your snorkeling gear, and get ready to immerse yourself in the beauty of Cozumel, Mexico!
